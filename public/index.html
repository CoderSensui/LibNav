About LibNav

LibNav is a smart library navigation system designed to help you find physical books instantly. It bridges the gap between digital searching and physical shelves.

How to Use

1
SearchFind your desired book via text or voice.
2
NavigateTap a book to view the step-by-step visual map to its shelf.
3
Companion ModeOn a PC? Scan the QR code to take the map on your phone.
Important Tip

Don't forget to formally check out the book at the front desk before leaving the library!
