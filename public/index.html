About LibNav

LibNav is a smart library navigation system designed to help you find physical books instantly. It bridges the gap between digital searching and physical shelves.

How to Use

1
Search Find your desired book via text input or voice recognition.
2
Navigate Tap a book cover to view the step-by-step visual map to its exact shelf.
3
Companion Mode If you are on a PC, simply scan the QR code to seamlessly take the map with you on your phone.
Important Reminder

Don't forget to formally check out the book at the front desk before leaving the library!
