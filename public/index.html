About LibNav

LibNav is an exclusive smart library navigation system built for Notre Dame - Siena College of General Santos City. It bridges the gap between our digital catalog and physical shelves, modernizing the campus reading experience.

How to Use
1
Search Find your desired book via text input or voice recognition.
2
Navigate Tap a book cover to view the step-by-step visual map to its exact shelf.
3
Companion Mode On a PC, scan the QR code to take the map with you on your phone.
Important Reminder Don't forget to formally check out the book at the front desk before leaving the library!
